This patient is a 48-year-old male who presented with a moderately differentiated squamous cell carcinoma of the tongue, diagnosed on 3/31/2013. The tumor was classified as T4a, indicating extensive local invasion, and was accompanied by N1 nodal involvement, requiring a selective neck dissection. Pathology revealed a moderately differentiated squamous cell carcinoma. Following surgical intervention, adjuvant therapy was initiated. The final follow-up on 5/29/2013 revealed no evidence of disease, suggesting successful treatment and management of the oral cancer.
