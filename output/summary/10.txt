This patient is a 25-year-old female presenting with a moderately differentiated squamous cell carcinoma of the tongue, diagnosed on 12/28/2015. The tumor was classified as T4a, indicating extensive local invasion, and was treated with a modified radical neck dissection. Pathological examination revealed no nodal involvement (N0). Following surgery, adjuvant therapy was administered. The last follow-up, conducted on 10/17/2021, reported no evidence of disease. The initial tumor was located on the tongue.
