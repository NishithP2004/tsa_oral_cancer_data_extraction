This patient is a 43-year-old male who presented with a moderately differentiated squamous cell carcinoma of the tongue, diagnosed on 11/14/2016. The initial tumor was classified as T2 based on clinical staging according to the 8th Edition. Following surgical resection involving a selective neck dissection (1-4 levels), the patient experienced a local recurrence on 7/1/2017. Subsequent pathological examination confirmed the recurrence with an N2c nodal staging according to the 8th edition, and a pathological T3 classification. Despite adjuvant therapy, the patient ultimately succumbed to the disease with disease progression, final pathological N staging was confirmed as N2a. The last documented follow-up was 11/25/2017.
