The patient is a 49-year-old male who underwent surgery on September 25, 2018, for a moderately differentiated squamous cell carcinoma located in the buccal mucosa. The initial surgical approach involved a selective neck dissection (1-4 levels), performed due to the buccal location of the tumor. Pathological examination revealed a T1, N0 staging according to the 8th Edition of the clinical tumor staging system. Importantly, the pathological nodal evaluation demonstrated no evidence of nodal involvement (N0). Subsequent follow-up on November 24, 2018, indicated no evidence of disease. The surgery was performed to treat a T1, N0 moderately differentiated squamous cell carcinoma of the buccal mucosa.
