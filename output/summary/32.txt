This patient is a 66-year-old male who presented with a floor of mouth oral squamous cell carcinoma diagnosed on 4/29/2016. The tumor was classified as moderately differentiated. Following surgical resection involving a selective neck dissection (levels 1-4), pathological examination revealed a T3 tumor stage and an N0 nodal status, aligning with the 8th Edition clinical staging system. Adjuvant therapy was subsequently administered. Most recently, at his last follow-up on 3/4/2022, there was no evidence of disease recurrence.
