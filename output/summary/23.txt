This 60-year-old female patient was diagnosed with moderately differentiated squamous cell carcinoma of the tongue, initially classified as T4a based on pathological findings from surgery performed on 12/30/2019. The tumor was treated with a selective neck dissection (1-4 levels). Pathological examination revealed no nodal involvement (N0). Following surgery and adjuvant therapy, there has been no evidence of disease detected at the last follow-up on 7/28/2021. The tumor was located sub-periosteally on the tongue.
