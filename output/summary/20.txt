This 54-year-old male patient presented with a moderately differentiated squamous cell carcinoma of the tongue, diagnosed on March 15, 2021. The tumor was classified as T4a, indicating a large tumor with potential for invasion into deeper structures, and N2c, signifying the involvement of two or three levels of lymph nodes in the neck. A selective neck dissection was performed. Following surgery, adjuvant therapy was initiated. Pathology revealed no further evidence of disease at the last follow-up on November 8, 2021.
