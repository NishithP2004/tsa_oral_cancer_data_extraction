The patient is a 47-year-old male who presented with a well-differentiated squamous cell carcinoma originating on the tongue. Surgical resection, including a selective neck dissection (levels 1-4), was performed on 3/22/2018. Pathology revealed a T3 tumor, classified as N2a nodal involvement according to the 8th Edition staging system. The initial surgical procedure was followed by adjuvant therapy. Subsequently, the patient experienced local recurrence, with nodal recurrence occurring as well. The final follow-up, dated 10/16/2019, indicated no evidence of disease. The surgery was performed bilaterally. At the time of last follow-up, the patient remained free of disease.
